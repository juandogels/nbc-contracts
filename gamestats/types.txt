There are currently a few types available (more can be added in the future):

0 = null (no type, usually for second element if NBMon only has one element)
1 = ordinary (normal)
2 = fire 
3 = water 
4 = electric
5 = earth (mixture of ground and rock)
6 = wind (flying)
7 = frost (ice)
8 = crystal (steel)
9 = nature (grass)
10 = brawler (fighting)
11 = spirit (ghost and dark combined)
12 = magic (fairy)
13 = psychic 
14 = reptile (dragon and bug combined)
15 = toxic (poison)
